The hydration calculator estimates your **daily water target** from your body
weight (~**35 ml per kg**) plus an allowance for your activity level.

<Callout type="tip">
  A practical check: apart from the first morning pee, your urine should be
  light/clear. Drink a glass on waking and one before each meal.
</Callout>

## Try it
