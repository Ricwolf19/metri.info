Base diets sit around **2,100 kcal**. The two levers that matter: **total
calories** and **total protein**.

## Adjust by goal

- **Lose fat (&gt;65 kg):** keep the plan, train as written, walk 8–10k steps.
- **Lose fat (&lt;65 kg):** drop each food amount ~**10%**.
- **Gain muscle:** keep the plan; if you don't gain weight in 2 weeks, add
  ~**10%**.

<Callout type="info">
  **Golden rule:** if you're progressing, change nothing. No progress after 2
  weeks → adjust 5–10%.
</Callout>
