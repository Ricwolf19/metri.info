The healthy-weight calculator shows your **BMI** (Body Mass Index = kg ÷ height²)
and the **healthy weight range** for your height.

## Reading the bands

The range applies the WHO healthy-BMI band (**18.5–24.9**) to your height.

| BMI         | Band        |
| ----------- | ----------- |
| Under 18.5  | Underweight |
| 18.5–24.9   | Normal      |
| 25–29.9     | Overweight  |
| 30+         | Obese       |

<Callout type="info">
  BMI ignores muscle, so very muscular people can read as "overweight" while
  being lean. Use it as a rough guide, not a verdict — pair it with the
  [body-fat calculator](/tools/body-fat-calculator).
</Callout>
