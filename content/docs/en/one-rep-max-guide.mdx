The 1RM calculator estimates your **one-rep max** (1RM) — the most you could
lift once — from a weight you lifted for several reps. Handy for setting working
weights without testing a true max.

## Two formulas

```
Epley:   1RM = weight × (1 + reps ÷ 30)
Brzycki: 1RM = weight × 36 ÷ (37 − reps)   # best under ~10 reps
```

<Callout type="tip">
  Accuracy drops above ~10 reps — use a set of **3–6 reps** for the best
  estimate.
</Callout>
